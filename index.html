Angel 7 is a dessert cafe whose goal is to provide both sweets and happiness for its customers. Currently only located in San Francisco, California, but hope to expand to multiple chains throughout the Bay Area. Angel 7 welcomes anyone who loves dessert, wants to take aesthetic photos to share on social media, or even for those who just want to chill and hangout in a cozy environment.
